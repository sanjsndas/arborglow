Case Studies

1. Urban Canopy Restoration Project (2018-2022): Worked with the city parks department to rehabilitate 200+ street trees suffering from compaction stress and nutrient deficiencies. Implemented a 4-year care plan that included soil aeration, targeted fertilization, and regular health assessments. The project resulted in a 75% improvement in tree vitality and a significant increase in canopy cover.

2. Historic Oak Preservation (2021): Collaborated with local historians to restore a 150-year-old oak tree in a public park. Our team conducted a thorough health assessment, implemented disease management strategies, and enhanced the tree's structural integrity through careful pruning. The tree now serves as a focal point for community events.

About ArborGlow

Founded in 2010, ArborGlow has grown from a small local tree care service to a recognized leader in urban forestry and arboriculture. Our team of ISA-certified arborists combines decades of experience with cutting-edge techniques to deliver exceptional results.

Our Philosophy

We operate on three fundamental principles:

1. Science-Based Care: Every recommendation we make is grounded in current arboricultural research and best practices. We stay at the forefront of tree care technology through continuous education and professional development.

2. Environmental Stewardship: We prioritize sustainable methods that protect the broader ecosystem. Our treatments are carefully selected to target specific issues while preserving beneficial organisms and minimizing runoff.

3. Artistic Vision: Trees are living sculptures that define outdoor spaces. Our pruning and lighting services enhance each tree's natural form while respecting its biological needs.

Community Involvement

Beyond our commercial services, ArborGlow is committed to urban reforestation and community education. Each year, we:

  • Donate hundreds of hours to care for trees in public parks and schools
  • Host free workshops on proper tree care techniques
  • Partner with local organizations on neighborhood tree planting initiatives
  • Provide pro bono services for historically significant trees

We believe that investing in our urban forest today creates a healthier, more beautiful city for future generations.
